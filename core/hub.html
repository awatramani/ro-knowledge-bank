JPAL SA | Research Operations

Transforming scattered institutional memory into a unified, actionable operational framework.

Fieldwork is inherently dynamic, but it should never be chaotic. This central repository bridges the critical gap between meticulous study design and unpredictable ground realities. We provide standardised, highly robust guidance on everything from ethical compliance and precise HR protocols to complex SurveyCTO server logistics.

Built not just from theoretical methodology, but by the very surveyors, coordinators, and research managers executing large-scale data collection. Here, you will access proven routing strategies, download field-ready templates, and leverage collective operational wisdom to decisively de-risk your deployment before Day 1.
